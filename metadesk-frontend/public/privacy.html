Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

This Privacy Policy describes how Hide & Style ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Juhdi, collects, uses, and protects information when customers interact with us through Facebook Messenger, Instagram Direct, WhatsApp, or our website. We use a platform called Jinn to manage customer communications. By messaging us, you agree to this policy.

1 Information We Collect

  • Messages & Conversations: Text, images, and files you send to us via Messenger, Instagram, or WhatsApp.
  • Platform Identity: Your name and platform user ID as provided by Facebook/Meta when you message us.
  • Message Metadata: Timestamps, read receipts, and platform identifiers.
  • Inquiry Details: Product questions, order information, or support requests you share voluntarily.

We do not collect payment information, passwords, or any data beyond what is necessary to respond to your messages.

2 How We Use Your Information

  • To read, manage, and respond to your customer support messages
  • To generate AI-assisted replies that are relevant to your question
  • To maintain a conversation history so we can provide context-aware support
  • To track and improve our customer service performance

3 AI-Powered Messaging

We use Artificial Intelligence to help our team respond to your messages faster and more accurately. Here's what that means for you:

  • Your message may be processed by an AI system to generate a draft reply
  • AI responses are grounded in our product and business information (FAQs, product catalog)
  • A human agent may review and edit AI responses before sending
  • You can always request a human agent by saying "speak to a person"
  • We do not use your conversations to train AI models

5 Meta Platform Compliance

Our use of Meta's APIs complies with Meta's Platform Terms and Meta's Privacy Policy. Specifically:

  • We only access messaging data to provide customer support
  • We do not use Meta platform data for advertising or tracking
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for their marketing purposes
  • Message data is used solely for responding to your inquiries

6 Data Retention

We retain conversation history for up to 12 months to provide consistent support. After this period, or upon your request, your data is permanently deleted from our systems.

7 Data Security

Your data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only. We use industry-standard security practices and our hosting providers (Supabase, Render) maintain SOC 2 compliance.

8 Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the conversation data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your message history at any time
  • Opt-out of AI: Request to be handled by a human agent only
  • Correction: Ask us to correct any incorrect information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

9 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be communicated through our messaging channels or this page. Continued use of our messaging services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
